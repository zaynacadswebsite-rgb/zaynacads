About Me

I'm an academic research consultant based in the Philippines, working with senior high school, college, and graduate students on thesis and research paper requirements. I help clients strengthen their research design, run and interpret statistical analysis, and polish their papers for submission and oral defense — using clear methodology and honest, collaborative guidance every step of the way.
